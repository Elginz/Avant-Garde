What is a Newsletter?

A newsletter is a regularly distributed publication that is generally about one main topic of interest to its subscribers. Newsletters can be delivered electronically via email, or in print via regular mail.

Newsletters are often used by businesses, organizations, and individuals to keep subscribers informed about upcoming events, new products or services, or to provide educational information on a particular topic.
