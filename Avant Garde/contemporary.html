Contemporary

Contemporary architecture is the movement where modern styles blend, sharing various features and relying on fewer classicized building ideas.

Designs

The Quadracci Pavilion (at the Milwaukee Art Museum)

Most visitors associate the Milwaukee Art Museum with the Quadracci Pavilion (2001), a fantastical, post-modernist, bird-like structure designed by Spanish architect Santiago Calatrava.Its most striking feature is the brise soleil, a moveable, wing-like sunscreen.

Centre Pompidou-Metz,France

The Centre Pompidou-Metz is the first offshoot of a major French cultural institution - the Centre Pompidou in Paris - in collaboration with regional authorities.Sibling to the Centre Pompidou, the Centre Pompidou-Metz is entirely independent in its scientific and cultural choices.

San Francisco Museum of Modern Art

When Mario Botta set out to design a new home for the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art in 1988, his goal was to create a landmark building with "iconic strength". Both admired and panned, the building opened in 1995 in the city's South of Market (SoMa) neighbourhood, a blighted area on the verge of a major construction boom.
